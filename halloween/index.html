History of Halloween

Halloween is one of the oldest traditions in the world. It is so old because it touches on an essential element of the relationship between the living and the dead. Every recorded civilization has created some form of observance centered around what happens to people when they die. While not all countries and places in the world celebrate Halloween like it is celebrated in America, they celebrate Halloween in one form or another. Halloween is always celebrated on October 31st and in 2022, it will land on a Monday. The tradition started from the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain where people would light bonfires and wear costumes to fight off ghosts.
